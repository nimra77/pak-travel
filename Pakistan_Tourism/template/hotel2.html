Rooms & Suites

Faisalabad’s rich personality and textile heritage is carefully woven into the design of each room. Lavish textures, hand-woven carpets and ornate woodwork are complemented by modern amenities designed to provide the utmost in elegance, comfort and convenience, including marble bathrooms, complimentary high-speed internet, minibars, 24-hour room service and flat screen TVs. Twin, queen-sized or king-sized beds are dressed in soft linens, while large soundproofed windows keep out any noise and allow plenty of natural light in. Most rooms afford inspiring views of the hotel’s gardens and patios.
